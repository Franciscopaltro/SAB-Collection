About the Brand

SAB Collection is founded by Bannor Yeboah Sarah, a Business Administration student at the University of Professional Studies, Accra. With practical experience in customer service, record management, and community leadership, she has built the brand around reliability, accountability, and a refined customer experience.

SAB Collection serves both men and women and focuses on stylish pieces that feel elevated, thoughtful, and memorable. The brand is not only about what you wear. It is about the full experience, from product selection to packaging, customer care, and delivery.

Installment Plan

How it works
Pay 50% upfront to secure your item. The balance must be cleared before pickup or delivery.

Duration
You have up to 3 weeks to complete payment. Orders not completed within this period will be cancelled.

Policy
The initial deposit is non refundable. Items are released only after full payment. No reduction applies to installment purchases.

Delivery Information

Deliveries are scheduled for Fridays through a dedicated rider. Express delivery is available at an additional cost, and Yango or Bolt delivery can also be arranged.

Within Kumasi, doorstep delivery ranges from 35 to 40 cedis. Delivery to VIP, OA, or STC parcel office is 8 cedis through the rider.

Meet ups or pickups may be arranged based on availability. Your patience and understanding are appreciated.

Why customers choose SAB Collection

Because the brand combines quality pieces, a soft luxury feel, reliable communication, and thoughtful service from order to delivery.

How to order

Send a message with the item you want, your size or preference, and your location. Orders are confirmed after payment and delivery arrangements are discussed clearly.
